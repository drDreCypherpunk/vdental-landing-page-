FAQ

Quick answers

Do dental implants hurt?

Implant placement is done under local anesthesia — most patients report minimal discomfort, comparable to a tooth extraction.

How long do dental implants last?

With proper care, implants can last 20+ years or a lifetime — a permanent, fixed solution.

Can I eat normally after implants?

Yes — once healed, implants function like natural teeth, letting you eat all foods comfortably.

What is the cost of dental implants?

Cost depends on the number of implants and treatment type. Your free consultation + CBCT scan gives an exact, transparent quote.

How many visits are required?

Most cases need 2-4 visits: consultation & scan, implant placement, and crown fitting — with same-day options for eligible patients.
